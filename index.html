About Me

Hi! I'm Maksym Zvarycz, a 3D artist and student based in Warsaw, Poland. I've been creating digital art and 3D models for about five years, and I'm currently studying HTML for creating my portfolio page

My main focus is hard-surface modelling and environment design. I like to create animated films, adverts and digital worlds. I dream of working at Pixar studio in future

I work primarily in Blender and Maya , with post-processing done in Photoshop and Premiere Pro. Every project I take on is a chance to learn something new.

When I'm not modelling, I enjoy playing games, watching sci-fi films, and studying concept art and animation . I'm currently open for freelance work and collaborations.
